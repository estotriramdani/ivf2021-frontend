BINA NUSANTARA started with a dream and a sense of determination. Founded by Joseph Wibowo Hadipoespito and Theresia Widia Soerjaningsih, this education institution has gone through many stages, and overcome various barriers. It still stands strong and grand despite the competition.

Based on our belief in God, our passion for education and our view of a bright future, we are committed to develop the nation and to building a global community through education and technology. This commitment is reflected in the values of BINA NUSANTARA which become the guidance of our actions, our values is striving for excellence, perseverance, integrity, respect, innovation, teamwork, and we called it SPIRIT.

BINUS is well-known and fast growing institution that has been recognized nationally and internationally. In 2019, it was awarded for the second time as the #1 Indonesian Employer of Choice in Education by Korn Ferry - SWA. We endeavor to achieve a conducive working & learning environment, with a family-friendly multicultural community.
