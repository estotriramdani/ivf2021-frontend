Company Profile

Coca-Cola Europacific Partners (CCEP) is one of the world's leading consumer goods companies. We manufacture, distribute, and sell some of the world's most beloved brands. We combine the strength and scale of a large multi-national business with local expertise and wisdom for the customers we serve and the communities we support. As a part of the global CCEP system, in CCEP Indonesia we operate eight manufacturing facilities in Sumatra, Java, and Bali employs a direct workforce of more than 5,500 people and distributes a million cases of refreshing drinks to more than 450,000 outlets across the nation.

At Coca-Cola Europacific Partners Indonesia, we believe that our people are the foundation of our success. It means that our long-term success, lies in creating a workplace that is safe, flexible, and inclusive for our people. With that strong belief and commitment, we continue our commitment to protect the safety and wellbeing of our people, engaging with our employees, recognizing their concerns, and taking the necessary steps to enable them to feel safe and secure in their jobs, especially during these unprecedented times. In addition to that, we also continues to develop a comprehensive capability development program to equip thousands of employees with the right knowledge and skills for their career development now and in the future.

Overall, we make sure that in every decision made, we deliver the best outcomes for our people, our customer and consumers, our environment, our communities as well as our shareholders. We will continue to build a workplace and teamwork that we can be proud of and an environment that allows us to be the best version of ourselves
