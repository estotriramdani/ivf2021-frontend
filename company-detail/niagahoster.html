Main Services

  • Unlimited Hosting
    Unlimited hosting is perfect for small and medium websites. A first-timer needs little to none technical knowledge to start. Affordable yet beginner friendly, the service becomes Niagahoster’s most best-selling web hosting plans
  • Cloud Hosting
    Cloud Hosting promises power and performance. The service fits customers who require large resources who fancy easy management, thanks to our compact dashboard.
  • Virtual Private Server (VPS)
    Virtual Private Server stimulates the experience of a dedicated server even though, technically, a user is still sharing the physical server with others. It’s perfect for advanced users and websites that require high resources.
  • Domain
    Domain name is a website’s address that also serves as one’s identity online. Niagahoster provides a huge library of domain extensions sucs as .com, .co.id, .id, .space, .website, .site, .online, and many others.
  • Email Hosting
    Email hosting is a service dedicated to sending and receiving emails. It also allows users to customize their email names, which works well for branding purposes. The service is ideal for business, community, and blog
  • WordPress Hosting and Managed WordPress
    WordPress Hosting is a service dedicated to WordPress users. Beginners are welcome as our integrated dashboard guarantees easy management
